# Code of Conduct

This repository is meant to support serious engineering, trading-system operations, and long-running automation. We expect communication to be calm, direct, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be specific about problems, tradeoffs, and evidence.
- Assume good intent and explain disagreements clearly.
- Keep reviews focused on behavior, risk, and implementation quality.
- Protect operator time by reporting issues in a reproducible way.
- Treat credentials, broker details, and security-sensitive material with care.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or hostile tone
- Sharing secrets, tokens, or private infrastructure details in public threads
- Low-effort bug reports that hide key context or reproduction steps
- Pressure to skip verification for changes that affect money movement, risk, or safety boundaries

## Working style

For this project, good collaboration means:

- product honesty over hype
- reproducibility over vague claims
- operational safety over speed theater
- clear docs over tribal knowledge

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, remove, or reject discussions and contributions that break these rules or create unnecessary operational risk.
